Transport

Transition your commute method

+

Moving around solo in combustion vehicles constitutes your largest carbon output. Swapping just 2 drives a week for electric vehicle carpooling, rail, or bike transits offers an aggressive reduction curve.

Diet

Introduce plant-based days

+

Red meats (beef and lamb) are heavily carbon-intensive. Committing to plant-based choices on weekdays and choosing local produce bypasses heavy supply emissions.

Potential annual savings
03
Energy

Audit residential thermostat loops

+

Heating and cooling standard grid-powered households drives hefty emissions. Lowering temperature thresholds by 1°C in winter and upgrading to smart plugs decreases base loads significantly.

-0.7 t
Potential annual savings

Adopt a circular consumption cycle

+

Fast fashion and electronics demand severe manufacturing and logistics carbon. Shift to a thrift-first mindset and repair appliances to intercept linear waste pipelines.
